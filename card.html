Hippopotamus

Hippopotamuses are found in Africa. The name hippopotamus means 'river horse' and is often shortened to hippo. The hippopotamus is generally considered the third largest land mammal (after the White rhinoceros and elephant).

  • Scientific Name: Hippopotamus
  • Average Length: 3.3 – 5.2 m
  • Average Lifespan: 40 – 50 years
  • Habitat: River, Swamp, Lake

The name comes from the ancient Greek for "river horse" (ἱπποπόταμος). After the elephant and rhinoceros, the common hippopotamus is the third-largest type of land mammal and the heaviest extant artiodactyl. ... The hippopotamus is among the most dangerous animals in the world as it is highly aggressive and unpredictable.
